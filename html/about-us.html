About Us

Last Wednesday, we visited Trader Joes at the “off-hour” of 2pm to avoid the excess risk that now comes with large crowds. After waiting in line, six-feet apart of course, for 30 minutes, we quickly remembered that societal norms have greatly shifted due to COVID-19.

Given this new way of life, we wanted to help society make informed decisions. In order to keep our friends and family as safe as possible, we need to know where the least busy grocery store is and when the local park is least crowded to walk the dog.

Uncrowd.me allows users to find the least busy times and places so that we can reduce exposure, minimize wait, and finally get our lives back.

We hope uncrowd.me helps you stay safe and adapt to these unprecedented times, and if any of your friends, co-workers, or family could benefit from uncrowd.me, please help them by sharing this with them!
